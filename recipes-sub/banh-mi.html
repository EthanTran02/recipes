Banh Mi recipe

Bánh Mì: Vietnam's Iconic Sandwich

A crusty baguette filled with savory meats, pickled vegetables, fresh herbs, and a burst of flavorful spreads. Bánh mì is a Vietnamese street food sensation that will leave you wanting more.

ingredients

  • Bread
  • Grilled Pork
  • Pork
  • Pâté
  • Cilantro

Steps

  • Prepare Your Ingredients: Marinate and cook your chosen protein (pork, chicken, beef, tofu). Pickle daikon and carrots. Gather your spreads (pâté, mayonnaise), herbs (cilantro), and vegetables (cucumber, jalapeños).
  • Toast the Baguette (Optional): Lightly toast the baguette for a warm and crispy texture. Slice it lengthwise, creating a hinge.
  • Assemble the Sandwich: Spread pâté and mayonnaise on the baguette halves. Layer your protein, followed by pickled vegetables, cilantro, cucumber, and jalapeños.
  • Serve Immediately: Enjoy your fresh Bánh Mì while the baguette is still crisp and the flavors are vibrant.
