Cơm Tấm: The Vietnamese Broken Rice Delight

Experience the unique texture and vibrant flavors of Cơm Tấm, a popular Vietnamese dish made with fractured rice grains. This hearty plate features broken rice topped with an array of savory delights, such as grilled pork chops, shredded pork skin, a fried egg, and a sweet and savory fish sauce dressing. It's a comforting and satisfying meal that captures the essence of Vietnamese home cooking.

ingredients

  • Broken Rice
  • Grilled Pork Chops
  • Shredded Pork Skin
  • Fried Egg
  • Fish Sauce Dressing

Steps

  • Cook the Broken Rice: Rinse the broken rice and cook according to package directions or in a rice cooker until tender.
  • Prepare the Pork Chops: Marinate pork chops in your preferred marinade (lemongrass, garlic, fish sauce are common). Grill or pan-fry until cooked through.
  • Prepare Shredded Pork Skin & Egg: Shred cooked pork skin and toss with roasted rice powder. Fry an egg to your liking.
  • ssemble & Serve: Place cooked rice on a plate. Top with grilled pork chop, shredded pork skin, and fried egg. Drizzle generously with fish sauce dressing and serve with desired vegetables.
